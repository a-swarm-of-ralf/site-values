Personalizing a Question

The question below is a boring factual question our chat bot can ask a person during their conversation. We want to alter this question to fit the personality of the person chatting with our chatbot.

The Question:

Do you agree or disagree with: Digital currencies, such as Bitcoin, are the future of spending online?

Please look a the personality described on the right and try to image how we would best ask somebody with that personality this question. Note that is always best to give a positive twist to the question even if the personality might seem negative.

User's Personality:

This user values power highly.

Users who value power care about authorty and wealth. They focus on social esteem and emphasize the attainment or preservation of a dominant position within the their social group.

Example

For example we have a user who has a personality trait value stimulation.

And we have a neutral question "Do you agree or disagree with: 'Banks should offer current accounts in cryptocurrency such as Bitcoin'".

In this example we would consider the personality trait of the user. User's who value stimulation like trying new things and finding novel experiences, so we could formulate our question to appeal to this.

The question would be: "Do you agree or disagree: I would like to try a bank account in crytocurrency.".
